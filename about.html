Why?



An influencer I'm inspired by lives by the mantra, "do what excites", which I think is a beautiful way to live. Recently, I've been realizing my own spin on the idea by "doing what I love".




What do I love?



Well, I love a lot of things. I'm extremely passionate about all things UX/UI. But besides that, I enjoy acting, reading, and calisthenics. I love spending time outside with my friends. I love to make things, even though it at times causes a lot of discomfort, and I, simply, love to learn. Most importantly, I'm very interested in breaking down the problems within the mental health and education spaces in our society today.




What's this for?



This portfolio showcases my work, mostly my experience working within UX/UI. But this portfolio is, more simply, a showcase of all the things I'm interested in and working on because that's the best representation of who I am.
